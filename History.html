THE HISTORY OF VESPA

75 years young

Since its appearance - in a country still to be rebuilt but also full of ideas, creativity and hope - Vespa has represented the joy of living and running towards the future and for this reason it soon became an icon of freedom and emancipation for girls and kids from all over the world. This richness in the values ​​of Vespa has been accompanied over the years by a style and technology that has always been at the forefront in the various decades that have passed.
